Bet selection

Conventionally a user’s bet selections were all or nothing, meaning if they didn’t achieve their target – they would win zero.

This user experience felt too stressful and the hypothesis was that users didn’t see value in the product, resulting in a less than desirable retention rate.

The concept of bounties was created to allow users to feel like they were taking less risk and even if they didn’t make a net profit, the experience wasn’t a total loss.

A few basic prototypes were tested to identify if potential users could understand the payout method.

Once a suitable design was identified an AB test was created along with the existing user experience.
